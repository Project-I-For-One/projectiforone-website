Our Story

Built to solve the problem. Not manage it.

Project I For One was founded in Los Angeles with one purpose: to create a world where homelessness is rare, brief, and non-recurring ‚Äî starting with one person at a time.

"Zero returns to homelessness ‚Äî that's our only metric."

We don't measure success by beds filled or services provided. We measure it by people who have a permanent home and never return to the street.

Our Origin

Why We Exist

Los Angeles has one of the most severe homelessness crises in the nation. Despite billions spent, the problem persists ‚Äî not because solutions don't exist, but because the system is broken by bureaucracy, temporary fixes, and a lack of accountability to real outcomes.


Project I For One was founded to cut through that. We operate lean, move fast, and stay laser-focused on what actually works: housing people immediately, then wrapping them with the support they need to stay housed permanently.


We're building from the ground up ‚Äî recruiting the right board, clinical partners, and street-outreach team ‚Äî because doing this right matters more than doing it fast.

Housing is a Human Right

Not a reward for sobriety, employment, or "program compliance." A right.

Dignity Before Everything

Every person we serve is treated with full dignity and respect ‚Äî no exceptions.

Results Over Reports

We are accountable to outcomes, not paperwork. If it doesn't house people permanently, we change it.

Built to Scale

Our pilot model is designed to prove itself in LA ‚Äî then expand to cities across the country.
