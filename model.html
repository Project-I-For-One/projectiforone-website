The model

Stop cycling people.
Start ending homelessness.

The system is optimized for process. We’re optimized for outcomes. Our approach is simple: house first, then deploy intensive support until stability is real.

1) Rapid rehousing

Move fast. Get keys. Stabilize immediately. Every day outside increases risk, cost, and trauma.

2) Intensive, personalized support

Support isn’t optional. We coordinate care, benefits, employment pathways, and crisis prevention.

3) Corridor targeting

We map high-need urban corridors and focus resources where they produce the biggest reduction in chronic street homelessness.

4) Accountability

We track outcomes aggressively. We’re guided by one metric: zero returns to homelessness.

We are not interested in “services delivered.”
Services are means, not ends. The end is permanent housing stability.
